Grabbelplatz, Kunstsammlung NRW

"Zeitraum (51° 13.66 N, 6° 46.523 E) deals with the events on "Grabbeplatz", the place in front of the Kunstsammlung NRW. This square is captured in a bird's eye view by a central camera. The installation translates the traces of people on the square into a virtual sculpture. This shape is permanently changing, generating spaces and holes dependend on the stored tracking data of the site.

The installation maps the presence of people and their moving speed on a self-organizing-map (a special neural network) which has been modified for the situation of a real place. The "modified "SOM" is interpreted by a Marching-Cube-Algorithmus to an isosurface.
