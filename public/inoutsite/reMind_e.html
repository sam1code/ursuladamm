Presentation of the movement of commuters on a public place
The technique of "billboarding", known from 3D Programms, is used to produce a provisional 3D Presentation of the calculated traces and tracks on a place.
It should provide the spectator with an immersive experience of the tracks of the people around him/herself. The presentation needs 4 holopro screen.

Proposal for an interactive Installation on a public place. A public place shall become stage of an interaction field, related to a Café or an exhibition space, consisting in modular, selflearning and selforganisating elements. With the self-learning furnitures, it shall make transparent the character of the site and the preferred kind of usage of the visitors.
